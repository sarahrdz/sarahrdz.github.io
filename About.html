About

During my time at the University of Oklahoma, I have been apart of the Pride of Oklahoma as an alto saxophone player and a brother of Kappa Kappa Psi, an honorary band service fraternity. I have spent the last four years playing and serving the Pride. I have loved being apart of the largest student organization on campus, I truly found my family through these organizations.

I have enjoyed my time at the University of Oklahoma and I am excited to see what the future holds for me. I have learned so much about myself and what I am capable of. I have learned that I enjoy working with data and new technologies. I am excited to see where my career takes me and I am excited to see what I can do with my skills.
